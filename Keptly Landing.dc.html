Everything starts on your dashboard

Finding your way

Keptly takes about
a minute to learn.

01

The bar at the bottom

Five core areas on the tab bar — Today, Watch, Projects, Vault and Finance. On desktop they're in the left sidebar, along with Home, Vehicles, Insurance, Medical and Family.

02

The + button adds anything

The floating button always adds whatever fits the screen you're on — a goal on Goals, a task inside a project, a document in the Vault.

03

Search finds it instantly

The magnifier searches across every module at once — a task, a policy number, a person, a bill. Everything, one search.

04

Settings holds your account

Download a backup, take the guided tour, and under Account & Sync, start Premium to sync across devices.

05

Emergency is always one tap

The red button opens Emergency Mode — contacts, medical info and your family plan — even with no signal.

We will never sell your data, show you ads, train AI on your information, or make your data inaccessible. Document scans are encrypted on your device — we cannot read them even if we wanted to. Your export is always one tap away. Cancel Premium anytime; your data stays.
